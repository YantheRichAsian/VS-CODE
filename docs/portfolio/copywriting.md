## 时期一：初笔 · The First Mark

**主题词**：初笔 / The First Mark
**时间背景**：印刷公司工作时期
**核心项目**：Pudding Cake 品牌视觉设计

---

### 项目：Pudding Cake Brand Identity

**中文简介**

> 这是一切的起点。
>
> 受委于一家以委内瑞拉蛋糕为主题的甜品品牌，我完成了从 Logo 造型到贴纸视觉的完整品牌识别设计。设计语言以简洁、时髦为核心——没有多余的装饰，只有最精准的形与色。
>
> 这份作品不只是一个 Logo，它是我对「品牌应该长什么样」的第一次认真回答。

**English Version**

> This is where it all began.
>
> Commissioned for a Venezuelan-inspired pudding cake brand, I developed a complete brand identity — from logo mark to sticker visuals. The design language was deliberate: clean, contemporary, with nothing superfluous.
>
> More than a logo, this was my first serious answer to the question: *What should a brand look like?*

**作品标签**：`Brand Identity` · `Logo Design` · `Print` · `Sticker Design`

## 时期二：破界 · Beyond the Page

**主题词**：破界 / Beyond the Page
**时间背景**：保健公司工作时期
**核心项目**：冰寒30周年庆活动视觉 · 社交媒体内容系列

---

### 项目 A：Bing Han 30th Anniversary — Event Visual Identity

**中文简介**

> 当设计从纸张走向舞台，一切都不同了。
>
> 为炳翰保健公司30周年庆典，我独立完成了活动全套视觉系统——包括宽达3499像素的 LED 大屏幕横幅、活动专属 Logo，以及现场 Photobooth 布景板。
>
> 这是我第一次将设计「放大」到真实空间中。灯光亮起的那一刻，屏幕上的每一个像素都成为了庆典的一部分。

**English Version**

> When design steps off the page and onto the stage, everything changes.
>
> For Bing Han's 30th anniversary celebration, I delivered a complete event visual system — a 3499px-wide LED screen banner, a bespoke event logo, and a full-scale photobooth backdrop.
>
> It was the first time I saw my work fill a room. When the lights came on, every pixel became part of the celebration.

**作品标签**：`Event Design` · `LED Visual` · `Large Format` · `Brand Application`

---

### 项目 B：Health & Wellness Social Media Series

**中文简介**

> 好的内容，不只是好看——它要让人停下来，读下去，然后相信你。
>
> 在保健公司期间，我为品牌创作了一系列社交媒体内容，涵盖产品功效科普（心脏、脑中风、伤口修复）、成分解析、以及本地创业机会推广。
>
> 从极简信息图到情感共鸣型内容，每一张图都是一次精准的沟通设计。

**English Version**

> Great content doesn't just look good — it makes people stop, read, and believe.
>
> During my time at the wellness company, I created a social media content series covering product benefits (heart health, stroke prevention, wound recovery), ingredient breakdowns, and local entrepreneurship campaigns.
>
> From minimalist infographics to emotionally resonant visuals, each post was a precise act of communication design.

**作品标签**：`Social Media` · `Infographic` · `Content Design` · `Health & Wellness`

## 时期三：锋芒 · Sharp Edge

**主题词**：锋芒 / Sharp Edge
**时间背景**：金融/贷款行业时期
**核心项目**：贷款品牌社媒内容矩阵 · 传单设计

---

### 项目 A：Financial Services Social Media Matrix

**中文简介**

> 在最难开口的话题上，设计必须更有力量。
>
> 金融与贷款，是一个充满信任门槛的行业。我为客户构建了一套完整的社媒内容矩阵，横跨情绪共鸣（财务压力、家庭困境）、教育科普（防诈骗指南、贷款流程）、产品转化（合法贷款、抵押方案）三大内容层。
>
> 超过50件作品，每一张都在做同一件事：**用设计建立信任，用内容推动行动**。

**English Version**

> When the subject is difficult, design must work harder.
>
> Finance and lending is an industry built on trust — and trust must be earned. I built a comprehensive social media content matrix spanning three layers: emotional resonance (financial stress, family struggles), education (anti-scam guides, loan process explainers), and conversion (legal lending, mortgage solutions).
>
> Over 50 pieces. Each one doing the same thing: **building trust through design, driving action through content**.

**作品标签**：`Content Strategy` · `Social Media` · `Financial Design` · `Copywriting` · `Local Market`

---

### 项目 B：Promotional Flyer Design

**中文简介**

> 一张好的传单，三秒内必须完成它的使命。
>
> 为本地按摩院设计的 A5 促销传单，在有限的版面内完成了信息层级的精准排布——主题、优惠、行动呼吁，一气呵成。简洁不是偷懒，是对读者时间的尊重。

**English Version**

> A great flyer has three seconds to do its job.
>
> Designed for a local wellness center, this A5 promotional flyer delivers a clear hierarchy within a constrained format — headline, offer, call-to-action, in one clean sweep.
>
> Simplicity isn't laziness. It's respect for the reader's time.

**作品标签**：`Print Design` · `Flyer` · `Promotional` · `Local Business`
