01 印刷公司时期 · Print Studio Era

初笔
The First Mark

这是一切的起点。受委于一家以委内瑞拉蛋糕为主题的甜品品牌，完成了从 Logo 造型到贴纸视觉的完整品牌识别设计。设计语言以简洁、时髦为核心——没有多余的装饰，只有最精准的形与色。

Commissioned for a Venezuelan-inspired pudding cake brand, I developed a complete brand identity — from logo mark to sticker visuals. The design language was deliberate: clean, contemporary, with nothing superfluous.

02 保健公司时期 · Wellness Company Era

破界
Beyond the Page

当设计从纸张走向舞台，一切都不同了。为炳翰保健公司30周年庆典，独立完成活动全套视觉系统——LED 大屏幕横幅、活动专属 Logo、现场 Photobooth 布景板。与此同时，主导品牌社群媒体内容运营，从品牌视觉识别设计到每周内容发布，全程一手包办。

When design steps off the page and onto the stage, everything changes. For Bing Han's 30th anniversary, I delivered a complete event visual system — and took full ownership of the brand's social media content operation, from visual identity design to weekly content publishing.

03 金融行业时期 · Financial Services Era

锋芒
Sharp Edge

在最难开口的话题上，设计必须更有力量。为金融贷款品牌构建完整社媒内容矩阵——情绪共鸣、教育科普、产品转化，三层并进。超过50件作品，每一张都在做同一件事：用设计建立信任，用内容推动行动。

When the subject is difficult, design must work harder. I built a comprehensive social media content matrix spanning emotional resonance, education, and conversion — over 50 pieces, each building trust through design and driving action through content.
